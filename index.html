Six Flags Fiesta Texas is a theme park near me, and has the roller coasters Goliath and Iron Rattler. Six Flags Fiesta Texas also has a water park and many family rides. Six Flags Fiesta Texas is part of the large chain of theme parks, Six Flags.

Aeroguard Flight School is a flights school in Georgetown TX. It is the flight school I hope to attend after going to college and is not really a tourist destination, but something I want to one day attend. The SpaceX Boca Chica site is a launch pad on the America-Mexico border that SpaceX plans to use for Starship Superheavy going to the moon and mars. I want to see this site both to watch takeoffs and landings and to see how construction is going on it right now.

This is where Boeing makes all of its planes, and even has its own runway. I want to go on a tour and possibly watch a 787 Dreamliner, their latest plane, be assembled.




Continent: Sunshine Village ski resort



As the name suggests, Sunshine Village is a ski resort in Canada. I don't particularly want to go there, I just searched "ski resorts" and it was one of the first ones to pop up. It is also at the highest elevation for a Canadian ski resort.




Western Hemisphere: Embraer Headquarters



Embraer is a South American company that makes small business and commercial jets. They have several lines and their largest is the Embraer E Series, which is their only series of planes that has wing mounted engines.




World: Beijing Daxing Airport



Beijing Daxing Airport is Beijing's new large international hub. It has 80 gates, 4 runways, and 5 concourses. It is shaped like a 6 pointed star and is very aestetically pleasing. It has 6 mega-columns at the start of each concourse and where the check in area ends.




Solar system: Titan



Titan is one of the largest moons in the solar system. It is possible that Titan could support life, because it has water. It is one of Saturn's moons and the most prominent feature of Titan is its hazy atmosphere. The atmosphere is mostly composed of nitrogen, with methane and ethane clouds.
